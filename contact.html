FAQ

Common Questions

Is MindEase free to use?

Yes! The core features of MindEase are completely free — mood tracking, basic AI chats, wellness tips, and community access. Premium unlocks additional features for £4.99/month.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted end-to-end. We are fully GDPR compliant. We never sell user data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Is MindEase a replacement for professional therapy?

No — MindEase is a supportive tool, not a clinical service. We always recommend professional therapy for serious mental health conditions. MindEase provides accessible daily support and bridges the gap between sessions.

How does the anonymous community work?

The community uses anonymous usernames — no real names, no photos required. Conversations are moderated by a combination of AI safety filters and human moderators to maintain a safe, supportive space.
