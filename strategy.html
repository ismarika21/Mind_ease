Marketing Strategy

How We Grow

A strategic, theory-backed approach to reaching, engaging, and retaining Gen Z users through data-driven marketing and ethical growth.

Academic Framework

STP Marketing Model

The STP framework guides our strategic focus — ensuring we reach the right people with the right message.

Segmentation

Dividing the market

Demographic

Age 16–25, students and early-career professionals, regardless of gender or location.

Psychographic

Digital-native, stress-affected, open to therapy alternatives, value privacy and authenticity.

Behavioural

Heavy mobile users, active on social media 4+ hours daily, interested in wellness content.

Geographic

Global – initially UK, US, Canada, and Australia with high English-speaking Gen Z populations.

Targeting

Choosing our focus

We use a concentrated targeting strategy — one clearly defined segment, served exceptionally well:

Primary Target
Students aged 18–22

experiencing stress/anxiety related to academic performance, experiencing digital burnout, and seeking affordable, private mental health support.

Why this segment? Highest anxiety rates, longest potential lifetime customer value, most influenced by peer recommendations and social proof.

Positioning

Our market stance

MindEase is positioned as the only mental health app that truly speaks Gen Z's language — affordable, anonymous, and AI-powered.

Position Statement

"For Gen Z students who feel overwhelmed, MindEase is the affordable, private mental health companion that uses AI to provide personalized support without judgment — unlike traditional therapy which is expensive, slow, and stigmatized."

Academic Framework

AIDA Communication Model

Every piece of MindEase communication is structured around the AIDA framework to guide users from awareness to action.

A

Attention – Grab their scroll

Channel: Instagram Reels & TikTok short videos featuring relatable Gen Z mental health content — "you're not alone" narratives, animated stress statistics, and viral awareness clips that stop the scroll within the first 2 seconds.

I

Interest – Nurture curiosity

Channel: Educational carousel posts about anxiety management, YouTube shorts on "how MindEase works," SEO blog articles targeting keywords like "mental health app for students" to sustain engagement after initial awareness.

D

Desire – Build emotional connection

Channel: User testimonials, before/after emotional wellness stories, influencer partnerships with Gen Z mental health advocates who authentically share their MindEase journey — creating emotional resonance and FOMO.

A

Action – Convert to users

Channel: Strong CTAs in all content — "Try MindEase Free," "No credit card needed," limited-time premium trials for early sign-ups, and in-app referral bonuses to drive organic viral growth among friend groups.

SMART Goals Framework

Measurable Targets

Every MindEase goal is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

S

Specific – Clear User Acquisition

Achieve 10,000 active registered users across UK, US, and Canada through organic social media, influencer partnerships, and SEO blog traffic within the first six months of launch.

M

Measurable – Trackable KPIs

Track monthly active users (MAU), daily mood logs, AI chat sessions, and weekly app opens. Target: 5,000 daily active users and 30% month-over-month engagement growth by Month 6.

A

Achievable – Realistic Growth

Based on competitor growth curves (Headspace acquired 8K users in first 3 months), 10K in 6 months is realistic with our concentrated Gen Z targeting and superior product-market fit.

R

Relevant – Aligned to Mission

User growth directly serves our mission of making mental health support accessible to more young people. Premium conversion provides revenue to fund product improvement and crisis support features.

T

Time-bound – Clear Deadlines

Month 1–2: 1,000 users (beta). Month 3–4: 4,000 users. Month 5–6: 10,000 users. Month 12: 30,000 users. Quarterly review of all KPIs with pivot strategy if targets missed by >20%.

Ethics & Responsibility

We Care Deeply

Data Privacy

All user data is encrypted end-to-end. We are fully GDPR compliant. We never sell, share, or monetize personal mental health data. Users can delete their account and all associated data at any time.

️

User Anonymity

MindEase allows full anonymous use — no real name required, no photo verification, no social media linkage. The anonymous community is moderated by AI + human moderators for safety, not identity.

Clinical Safety

MindEase is not a replacement for clinical therapy. Content is reviewed by licensed psychologists. Users in crisis are immediately directed to professional emergency resources and helplines.
